About Us

Experience the northern coast of Crete from your own private resort. With all the amenities of a luxury home, the Pivko Holiday Village offers the ideal situation to ensure a relaxing vacation.

Located between the scenic fishing villages of Milatos and Sisi, the Village is a 30-minute drive from the Heraklion Airport.

The Village area spans 12 dunams (approximately 3 acres) and includes two pools (one exclusively part of the Villa), tennis and basketball courts, soccer fields, a ping pong table, and more.

The private Villa is separated from the other holiday units by an electric gate and a wide soccer field ensuring complete privacy.

The entire complex is equipped with security cameras and perimeter lighting.
