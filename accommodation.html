Luxury Villa

The resort features a spacious 200-square-meter villa spanning two floors with a fenced observation roof, set on 0.5 acres. The villa includes 5 bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms, and a fully equipped kitchen with a refrigerator, oven, electric and gas stovetops, heating plate, microwave, dishwasher, espresso machine, toaster, juicer, electric kettle, and more. The kitchen is fully stocked with dinnerware for 12 guests, including wine glasses, shot glasses, and champagne glasses.

The living room offers a large sofa, two armchairs, a sitting couch, a carpet, a TV, and a speaker system. Four bedrooms have double beds, while one has two single beds. Each room features air conditioning (heating and cooling), three with ceiling fans, along with TVs, remote controls, closets, rugs, and reading lamps. All windows and doors have protective screens against insects.

The villa grounds include a professional bar with an attached bathroom and toilet, equipped with a large refrigerator, oven, microwave, additional refrigerators, and a full set of utensils.

Outdoor amenities feature a private swimming pool (heating available for an additional fee), a jacuzzi for 8 guests, and multiple lounge areas, including a built-in seating circle for campfires.

The villa offers private gated parking for two cars with automatic lighting and is fully secured with alarm systems, security cameras, and floodlights.
